Court booking fees vary depending on the sport and location, typically ranging from AED 50 to AED 500 per hour.

Can I book a sports court in Dubai for a private event?

Yes, many venues offer private bookings for events, corporate matches, and tournaments with customized packages.

Are there any free sports courts available in Dubai?

Some public parks and community centers offer free or low-cost access to sports courts, but availability is limited.
